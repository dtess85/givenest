# Givenest — Project Brief

---

## Concept

Givenest is a real estate brokerage where every home sale funds a cause. At closing, Givenest donates to a charity of the client's choice at closing. The amount comes from the standard commission of the client's choice. No deductions, no admin fees, no extra cost to the client. The giving comes out of the standard commission structure that was always there.

The business operates as a licensed Arizona brokerage with two models:

**Direct (Arizona):** Givenest agents represent buyers and sellers directly. The founding agents are also business owners — full commission flows into the business, owner compensation paid as draws.

**Partner Network (National):** Independent agents join to receive leads. At closing they pay a standard 30% referral fee — industry norm for referred business. Givenest passes 100% of that fee to Givenest. The partner network is how the model scales without proportional overhead.

Givenest donates directly to the client's chosen charity at closing. The full amount, every time.

## Legal Structure

### Givenest Donates Directly
Givenest donates directly to the client's chosen charity at every closing.

- Donates 100% of the giving amount from every closing — AZ direct and partner network
- Paid directly to the charity designated by the client
- Matching contributions and tax receipts handled via a licensed 501(c)(3) when introduced
- Full giving history published publicly on the platform

**Legally:** Donations are made directly by Givenest from commission income. A Foundation will be established as giving volume grows and the structure warrants it.

**Matching:** Voluntary client optional personal contributions and their tax treatment will be covered in the FAQ. They are not part of the base giving model.

## Business Model

### The Founding Commitment: 100% to Charity

The giving pool is never touched for operations. 100% of the 30% giving amount is donated directly to the client's chosen charity. No deductions.

This is a strategic choice as much as a generous one. "100% to charity" is a headline, a trust signal, and a marketing asset that no competitor can easily replicate. Charities receiving full distributions have genuine incentive to promote the platform to their donor bases — a compounding organic referral channel.

**The giving math:**

| | Calculation | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Agent commission | sale price × 2.5% | e.g. $13,750 on $550K home |
| Giving pool | commission × 30% | $4,125 |
| Givenest donation | giving pool × 100% | $4,125 donated to charity |

```
$500K  home  →  $3,750 to charity
$750K  home  →  $5,625 to charity
$1.2M  home  →  $9,000 to charity
```

### How Operations Are Funded

Operations are funded entirely from brokerage income and platform revenue — never from the giving pool — the giving pool goes entirely to charity.

**Phase 1 — AZ Brokerage Income (Y1–2)**
Givenest earns the full commission on AZ direct deals. The 70% of commission not donated to charity funds operations. Owner draws are variable — only paid when deals close.

**Phase 2 — Ancillary Platform Revenue (Y3–4)**
As volume and traffic grow: mortgage referrals, title and escrow partnerships, home insurance leads, moving services, home services marketplace. High-margin streams that scale with traffic, not just closings.

**Phase 3 — Optional Subscriptions (Y4+)**
Premium tiers for agents and charities. Never a gate — free participation always exists.

- **Agent premium ($99/mo):** Priority placement, performance dashboard, marketing toolkit
- **Charity premium ($199/mo):** Featured placement, impact reporting, co-branded content, quarterly donor reports

### Matching Donations — Voluntary Client Contribution

At closing, clients are invited to personally match the Givenest donation from their own funds. Entirely optional. Their contribution goes directly to Givenest and is tax-deductible.

```
$550K home example:
  Givenest donation:  $4,125  (Givenest's contribution — no client receipt)
  Client match (optional):  $4,125  (client's own funds — tax-deductible receipt issued)
  Total to charity:         $8,250
```

Never pre-checked, never assumed. Presented as an invitation at closing.

## Platform Value Thesis

The transaction model is a real, profitable business. The larger long-term value is the platform if traffic reaches meaningful scale.

**The audience:** Every buyer and seller on Givenest is high-intent and in the middle of the largest financial transaction of their life. Mortgage lenders, title companies, and insurers pay significantly to be in front of this user at exactly the right moment.

**What significant traffic unlocks:**
- Mortgage referrals — $1,000–2,000 per funded loan
- Title & escrow — preferred partner pays for placement or revenue-shares on premiums
- Home insurance — every buyer needs a policy before closing
- Home services marketplace — scales with visitors, not just closings
- Charity network — 1.8M nonprofits, documented giving history, no direct comparable

**The flywheel:** Every Givenest donation is a shareable moment — the charity announces it, the client shares it, the agent posts it. Organic word-of-mouth at scale.

**Valuation range (Year 5):**

| Scenario | Basis | Value |
|---|---|---|
| Transaction-only | 4x revenue | ~$7–8M |
| Platform model | 6x revenue | ~$16M |
| Platform model (bull) | 10x revenue | ~$27M |
| Strategic acquisition | Mission/brand premium | 2–3x operating value |

## The Platform

### 1. Home
Cumulative impact stats, How It Works (3 steps), live closing feed, featured charities, CTAs to buy or sell.

### 2. Buyer Portal
MLS property search → property detail page with Giving Panel → agent matching.

### 3. Seller Portal
Home value input → Givenest donation estimate → charity selection → agent matching.

### 4. Charity Showcase
Searchable directory of charities that have received Givenest Givenest donations. Free profiles with distribution totals, closing counts, and impact stories. Optional premium tier for featured placement.

### 5. Charity Search & Designation
Open 501(c)(3) search across 1.8M+ US nonprofits via IRS API or Every.org. Client picks any charity — no pre-vetting. Selection saved to transaction. Post-closing, Givenest validates EIN internally. Client receives a post-closing confirmation email. Charity designation is locked at closing — changes are handled by Givenest on the backend if needed.

### 6. Partner Agent Portal
Agents sign up free, list their profile and market areas, receive matched leads, and pay a standard 30% referral fee at closing via a per-transaction referral agreement generated before the client introduction.

### Giving Panel
Lives on every property detail page. Shows:
- List price → commission (2.5%) → Givenest donates (30% of commission, 100% to charity)
- Charity typeahead search
- Optional match prompt once charity is selected
- "Get Matched with an Agent" CTA

```js
export const COMMISSION_RATE  = 0.025;
export const GIVING_POOL_RATE = 0.30;

export const calcCommission   = (price) => price * COMMISSION_RATE;
export const calcGivingPool   = (price) => calcCommission(price) * GIVING_POOL_RATE;
export const calcDistribution = (price) => calcGivingPool(price); // 100% donated to charity
```
